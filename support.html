Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I use the CleanMyArea website?

To use the CleanMyArea website, simply register or log in, select the appropriate dustbin category (biodegradable, recyclable, or non-recyclable), and follow the instructions to submit your waste details. You can also explore resources on recycling and waste management in the 'Learn Recycling' section.
