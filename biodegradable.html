Green Dustbin

Biodegradable waste refers to waste that can be decomposed by bacteria or other natural organisms and not harm the environment. The green dustbin is designed specifically for such waste, ensuring it is disposed of in an eco-friendly manner.

How to Use the Green Dustbin

Only place biodegradable waste in the green dustbin. This includes organic materials that can decompose naturally. Avoid placing non-biodegradable items in this bin, as they cannot be processed in the same way and may harm the environment.

Items Accepted in the Green Dustbin

  • Vegetable & Fruit Peels
  • Food Scraps
  • Garden Waste (Leaves, Grass Clippings)
  • Paper & Cardboard
  • Wood
  • Eggshells
  • Coffee Grounds & Tea Bags
