Blue Dustbin

Recyclable waste includes materials that can be processed and reused, rather than being disposed of as garbage. The blue dustbin is specifically designed for these materials, helping to conserve resources and reduce environmental impact.

How to Use the Blue Dustbin

Place only recyclable items in the blue dustbin. These are materials that can be reprocessed into new products. Avoid placing non-recyclable or hazardous items in this bin to ensure proper recycling and avoid contamination.

Items Accepted in the Blue Dustbin

  • Plastic Bottles
  • Glass Bottles and Jars
  • Aluminum Cans
  • Paper (Newspapers, Magazines)
  • Cardboard (Cereal Boxes, Shipping Boxes)
  • Metals (Food Cans, Tin Cans,Irons)
  • Empty Aerosol Cans
